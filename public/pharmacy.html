Paracetamol Strength & Pediatric Dosage Matrix

Paracetamol (Acetaminophen) is formulated in different concentrations depending on age. Always administer by weight, not age, for pediatric patients.

Infant Oral Drops

Strength: 100 mg / mL

**Age**: Newborns to 1 Year (weight: 3.2 to 10 kg). Dosage: 10-15 mg/kg per dose. Typically administered via oral syringe.

Pediatric Suspension 120

Strength: 120 mg / 5 mL

**Age**: 1 to 5 Years (weight: 10 to 20 kg). Dosage: 10-15 mg/kg per dose. 5mL contains 120mg of active Paracetamol.

Pediatric Suspension 250

Strength: 250 mg / 5 mL

**Age**: 6 to 12 Years (weight: 20 to 40 kg). Dosage: 10-15 mg/kg per dose. Designed for older children.

Adult Tablets (500 / 650 / 1000)

Strength: 500 mg, 650 mg & 1000 mg

**Age**: 12+ Years & Adults. Dosage: 500mg or 650mg (Dolo) every 4-6 hours. Max: 4000 mg in 24 hours. 1000mg used for severe post-op pain.

Dolo 650

Active: Paracetamol (Acetaminophen)
Tablet
Chemical Composition

Paracetamol IP 650 mg.

Clinical Uses

High fever, severe body aches, mild-to-moderate pain, and fever associated with viral infections (including Dengue and Influenza).

Standard Dosage

**Adults**: 1 tablet every 6 hours as needed. Max: 4 tablets (2600mg) in 24 hours. (Absolute max for Paracetamol is 4000mg/day).

Precautions

Do not take with other Paracetamol-containing products. Avoid alcohol. High doses can cause acute liver failure.

Aspirin

Active: Acetylsalicylic Acid
Tablet
Chemical Composition

Acetylsalicylic Acid 75 mg (low-dose) to 325 mg (standard).

Clinical Uses

Low-dose (75mg) is used as a blood thinner to prevent heart attacks and stroke. Standard dose (325mg) is used for pain and fever relief.

Standard Dosage

**Cardiac Prevention**: 75mg - 150mg once daily. **Pain/Fever**: 325mg - 650mg every 4 hours. Take with water after meals.

Precautions

Never give to children/teenagers (risk of Reye's Syndrome - fatal brain/liver swelling). Contraindicated in bleeding disorders and Dengue.

Combiflam

Active: Ibuprofen + Paracetamol
Tablet
Chemical Composition

Ibuprofen BP 400 mg + Paracetamol IP 325 mg.

Clinical Uses

Severe musculoskeletal pain, arthritis, dental pain, painful menstrual cramps, and inflammatory joint swelling.

Standard Dosage

**Adults**: 1 tablet 2-3 times daily after food. Do not take on an empty stomach to prevent gastric irritation.

Precautions

Contraindicated in Dengue Fever (Ibuprofen thins blood and increases internal bleeding risks). Avoid in patients with gastric ulcers.

Tramadol

Active: Tramadol HCl
Tablet
Chemical Composition

Tramadol Hydrochloride IP 50 mg.

Clinical Uses

Opioid Analgesic: Management of moderate to severe acute or chronic pain (post-surgical pain, severe injury, or chronic cancer pain).

Standard Dosage

**Adults**: 50 mg - 100 mg every 4 to 6 hours. Max: 400 mg per day. Swallow whole; do not crush.

Precautions

Highly habit-forming (addiction risk). Can cause severe drowsiness, dizziness, and respiratory depression. Do not mix with alcohol.

Etoricoxib

Active: Selective COX-2 Inhibitor
Tablet
Chemical Composition

Etoricoxib IP 60 mg, 90 mg, or 120 mg.

Clinical Uses

Acute gouty arthritis pain, osteoarthritis, rheumatoid arthritis, and post-dental surgery pain.

Standard Dosage

**Osteoarthritis**: 60 mg once daily. **Acute Gouty Arthritis**: 120 mg once daily (limit to maximum 8 days treatment).

Precautions

Contraindicated in patients with uncontrolled hypertension, ischemic heart disease, or peripheral arterial disease.

Serratiopeptidase

Active: Proteolytic Enzyme
Tablet
Chemical Composition

Serratiopeptidase IP 10 mg (enteric-coated).

Clinical Uses

Reduces swelling, edema, and inflammation associated with post-traumatic injuries, surgeries, or localized infections.

Standard Dosage

**Adults**: 1 tablet (10 mg) three times daily, taken on an empty stomach (1 hour before or 2 hours after meals).

Precautions

Can increase bleeding risk. Discontinue 2 weeks prior to scheduled surgery. Avoid if taking blood thinners (Warfarin).

Calpol Drops

Active: Paracetamol
Newborn Drops
Chemical Composition

Paracetamol IP 100 mg per 1 mL.

Clinical Uses

Fever and mild pain in newborns and infants, especially post-vaccination fever.

Standard Dosage

**Newborns (3.2 - 10 kg)**: 10 - 15 mg/kg per dose. E.g., for a 5kg baby, dose is 0.5 mL to 0.75 mL. Repeat every 6 hours if needed.

Precautions

Always use the calibrated dropper provided. Do not dose by age alone; dose strictly by the baby's current weight.

Colicaid Drops

Active: Simethicone + Oils
Newborn Drops
Chemical Composition

Simethicone USP 40 mg + Dill Oil 0.005 mL + Fennel Oil 0.0007 mL per 1 mL.

Clinical Uses

Infantile colic, abdominal griping pain, gas distension, and flatulence in newborns and infants.

Standard Dosage

**Infants (under 6 months)**: 5 - 10 drops, 4 times daily, 15 minutes before feeding. **6-12 months**: 10 - 20 drops.

Precautions

Shake well before use. Do not exceed the recommended frequency. If colic persists, consult a pediatrician.

Calpol 250

Active: Paracetamol
Pediatric Syrup
Chemical Composition

Paracetamol IP 250 mg per 5 mL.

Clinical Uses

Fever and pain relief in older children (teething, viral fever, mild muscle aches).

Standard Dosage

**Children (15 - 40 kg)**: 10 - 15 mg/kg per dose. E.g., for a 20kg child, dose is 5 mL (250mg) every 6 hours. Max: 4 doses in 24 hours.

Precautions

Ensure you are using the 250mg/5ml strength and not the weaker 120mg/5ml strength. Check the bottle label carefully.

Piperazine Citrate

Active: Antihelminthic
Pediatric Syrup
Chemical Composition

Piperazine Citrate IP 750 mg per 5 mL.

Clinical Uses

Treatment of intestinal worm infections, specifically Roundworms (Ascaris lumbricoides) and Threadworms/Pinworms.

Standard Dosage

**Infants (under 2 years)**: 2.5 mL (375mg) as a single dose. **Children 2-6 Years**: 5 mL (750mg) as a single dose. Repeat after 2 weeks.

Precautions

Contraindicated in children with epilepsy, neurological disorders, or impaired renal function.

Phenobarbitone

Active: Anticonvulsant
Pediatric Syrup
Chemical Composition

Phenobarbitone Sodium IP 30 mg per 5 mL.

Clinical Uses

Control and prevention of generalized tonic-clonic seizures, febrile convulsions, and neonatal seizures in infants.

Standard Dosage

**Pediatrics**: 3 - 5 mg/kg per day in 2 divided doses. Highly individualized. Must be measured using a syringe.

Precautions

Highly sedative. Can cause respiratory depression, cognitive slowing, and behavioral disturbances in children. Long-term use requires monitoring.

Cremaffin

Active: Liquid Paraffin + Milk of Magnesia
Adult Syrup
Chemical Composition

Liquid Paraffin IP 1.25 mL + Milk of Magnesia IP 3.75 mL per 5 mL.

Clinical Uses

Symptomatic relief of acute and chronic constipation. Softens stool and eases bowel movements in pregnant women or geriatric patients.

Standard Dosage

**Adults & Elderly**: 15 mL - 30 mL (1 to 2 tablespoons) taken at bedtime with a glass of warm water.

Precautions

Avoid prolonged daily use (can cause dependence and nutrient malabsorption). Do not take within 2 hours of other oral medications.

Ascoril LS

Active: Levosalbutamol + Expectorants
Adult Syrup
Chemical Composition

Levosalbutamol 1 mg + Ambroxol Hydrochloride 30 mg + Guaiphenesin 50 mg per 5 mL.

Clinical Uses

Productive (wet) cough associated with asthma, bronchitis, COPD, and chest congestion.

Standard Dosage

**Adults**: 10 mL three times daily. **Children (6-12 Years)**: 5 mL three times daily.

Precautions

May cause mild tremors, increased heart rate (tachycardia), or palpitations due to Levosalbutamol. Caution in hypertensive patients.

Monocef

Active: Ceftriaxone Sodium
IV / IM Injection
Chemical Composition

Ceftriaxone Sodium Sterile USP 1g (1000 mg) per vial.

Clinical Uses

Severe bacterial infections including typhoid fever, bacterial meningitis, abdominal sepsis, and surgical infection prophylaxis.

Standard Dosage

**Adults**: 1g - 2g dissolved in sterile water, administered slowly via Intravenous (IV) infusion or deep Intramuscular (IM) injection once daily.

Precautions

Must be administered by a registered nurse or doctor. Test dose recommended to check for cephalosporin/penicillin anaphylaxis.

Lignocaine 1%

Active: Local Anaesthetic
Local Injection
Chemical Composition

Lignocaine Hydrochloride IP 1% w/v (10 mg/mL).

Clinical Uses

Production of local anesthesia (infiltration, nerve block, epidural) for minor surgical procedures, dental work, or suturing.

Standard Dosage

**Infiltration**: Individualized by procedure. Max single dose: 4.5 mg/kg (not exceeding 300 mg total without Epinephrine).

Precautions

Accidental intravenous injection can cause severe cardiovascular depression, bradycardia, arrhythmias, or seizures.

Thyronorm

Active: Levothyroxine Sodium
Tablet
Chemical Composition

Levothyroxine Sodium IP available in 12.5 mcg, 25 mcg, 50 mcg, 75 mcg, 100 mcg, 125 mcg, and 150 mcg.

Clinical Uses

Replacement therapy in hypothyroidism (underactive thyroid gland) and treatment of thyroid stimulating hormone (TSH) suppression.

Standard Dosage

**Adults**: Dose is highly individualized. Taken as a single dose **on an empty stomach, 1 hour before breakfast** with water.

Precautions

Do not take with calcium or iron supplements (reduces absorption). Monitor TSH levels every 6-8 weeks to adjust dosage.

Glycomet

Active: Metformin HCl
Tablet
Chemical Composition

Metformin Hydrochloride IP 500 mg, 850 mg, or 1000 mg.

Clinical Uses

First-line treatment of Type 2 Diabetes Mellitus to manage blood glucose, improve insulin sensitivity, and protect renal functions.

Standard Dosage

**Adults**: 500 mg twice daily or 1000 mg once daily, taken **with or immediately after meals** to minimize gastrointestinal upset.

Precautions

Contraindicated in severe renal impairment (eGFR < 30). Suspend medication 48 hours prior to any medical scans requiring contrast dyes.

Alprazolam

Active: Benzodiazepine (Anxiolytic)
Tablet
Chemical Composition

Alprazolam IP 0.25 mg or 0.5 mg.

Clinical Uses

Short-term management of severe anxiety disorders, panic disorder (with or without agoraphobia), and anxiety-associated insomnia.

Standard Dosage

**Anxiety**: 0.25 mg to 0.5 mg, administered 3 times daily. **Elderly**: Start with lowest dose (0.125 mg to 0.25 mg) 2 times daily.

Precautions

Highly habit-forming; physical and psychological dependence develops rapidly. Causes severe drowsiness. Do not discontinue abruptly (withdrawal seizures).

Allopurinol

Active: Xanthine Oxidase Inhibitor
Tablet
Chemical Composition

Allopurinol IP 100 mg or 300 mg.

Clinical Uses

Prevention of gout attacks, hyperuricemia (excess uric acid), and prevention of calcium oxalate kidney stones.

Standard Dosage

**Adults**: Start at 100 mg once daily, gradually titrating up to 300 mg daily based on serum uric acid levels. Take after meals.

Precautions

Discontinue immediately if any skin rash or hives develop (risk of Stevens-Johnson Syndrome - a life-threatening skin reaction).

Methotrexate

Active: Antimetabolite (DMARD)
Tablet
Chemical Composition

Methotrexate IP 5 mg, 7.5 mg, or 10 mg.

Clinical Uses

First-line disease-modifying drug (DMARD) for severe active Rheumatoid Arthritis, severe Psoriasis, and various oncology indications.

Standard Dosage

**RA & Psoriasis**: 7.5 mg to 15 mg taken **ONCE WEEKLY** on a designated day (never take daily). Co-prescribed with Folic Acid.

Precautions

Daily dosing error is fatal. Requires regular liver function tests (LFTs) and complete blood counts (CBC). Teratogenic (do not use in pregnancy).

Diazepam

Active: Benzodiazepine
Tablet
Chemical Composition

Diazepam IP 5 mg or 10 mg.

Clinical Uses

Management of acute anxiety, muscle spasms, acute convulsive seizures/epilepsy, and relief of acute alcohol withdrawal symptoms.

Standard Dosage

**Adults**: 2 mg to 10 mg, administered 2 to 4 times daily depending on severity. **Muscle Spasm**: 5 mg - 10 mg at night.

Precautions

Significant sedative properties; avoid driving or operating machinery. High risk of dependence. Contraindicated in respiratory depression.

Mebendazole

Active: Antihelminthic
Tablet
Chemical Composition

Mebendazole IP 100 mg or 200 mg.

Clinical Uses

Treatment of single or mixed gastrointestinal worm infestations (e.g., Pinworms, Roundworms, Whipworms, and Hookworms).

Standard Dosage

**Pinworms**: 100 mg as a single dose. **Other Worms**: 100 mg twice daily (morning and evening) for 3 consecutive days. Chew before swallowing.

Precautions

Contraindicated in pregnant women (teratogenic in animal studies). Not recommended for children under 2 years of age.
